Collect rewards on a grid task.
--------------

The experiment:

	In this experiment, participants are required to learn and follow a sequence of rewards on a grid. There are 4 rewards that are placed on 4 locations on a 3 x 3 grid. Participants first observe the sequence and then are required to navigate to the reward by pressing the up/down/left/right (or bottonbox) keys. 



Analysing your data:

	Data is stored in the excel file produced by PsychoPy in the data folder. response.corr column shows 1 for correct response and 0 for incorrect responses. response.rt column includes the reaction time for each trial.
